about the jean west.
Jeanswest Australian clothing brand began in 1972 with the opening of its first store by Alister Norwood. Over 45 years, Jane West has become one of the most popular clothing brands in Australia and Asia and has a wide variety of menswear, womenswear, childrenswear, accessories, bags and shoes. The first Jane West branch in the Middle East was opened in Dubai, and two years later, the second store in this region and the first Jane West branch in Tehran opened in October 2008. To date, this brand has opened the largest number of stores in the Retail Fashion market in Tehran and in the big cities of Iran. The goal of Jane West collection as a retailer in the fashion and casual clothing sector is to create relationships and create value for customers. Jane West's responsibility towards its family members, attention to details, continuous effective communication, creating a sense of emotional attachment have been the characteristics of this brand over the years, and it is still evolving with the participation of its members.
